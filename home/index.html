A coastal race weekend built for runners, families, and first-timers

Run along Yeppoon's coastline, finish by the lagoon, and enjoy a friendly regional festival atmosphere across two days of events. Whether you're chasing a marathon, stepping up to 10km, running with family, or supporting from the sidelines, race weekend is designed to feel clear, welcoming, and easy to navigate.

  • Scenic coastal courses
  • Distances for every runner
  • Festival atmosphere in the heart of Yeppoon
