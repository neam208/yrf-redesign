Make a weekend of it

The page becomes more useful when the destination story is grouped into clear travel reasons, not one long block of copy.

Coastal scenery

From Yeppoon Main Beach to quieter coves, the coast gives runners a relaxed place to unwind before and after race day.

Reef access

Yeppoon is a natural launch point for Keppel Islands adventures, snorkelling, diving, and reef-style day trips.

Town culture

Cafes, local shops, street art, community events, and relaxed hospitality give supporters plenty to explore.

Outdoor adventure

Byfield, walking tracks, caves, lookouts, and coastal drives make the region bigger than a race weekend.

Places to stay

Beachfront stays, family accommodation, resorts, apartments, and quieter retreats support different race-weekend needs.

Nature close by

Wildlife, island landscapes, bushland, beaches, and reef ecosystems make recovery days feel like part of the event.

Race, recover, explore

For travelling runners, the destination page should answer a quiet but important question: is this event worth turning into a trip? This layout makes the answer feel obvious.

  • Beach and lagoon recovery options after racing
  • Island and reef day trips for supporters and families
  • Local food, cafes, accommodation, and coastal drives

Plan around race weekend

A runner-focused travel panel gives visitors practical next steps instead of only scenic inspiration.

Arrive early, stay longer

Build in time to collect race packs, get familiar with the coastline, enjoy a shakeout walk, and recover properly after the event.

For the broader destination guide, link visitors directly to Visit Capricorn for accommodation, food, transport, and itinerary planning.
