Race packs

Collection
Do I need to collect bibs for the Family Fun Run?

Yes. All race packs must be collected during bib collection times. There is no bib collection available Saturday at the race. If you require further assistance, please contact us.

View race pack collection details

Can someone else collect my race pack on my behalf?

Yes. Someone else can collect your race pack on your behalf, but they will need a copy of your race confirmation. Please make sure they have this documentation when they arrive.

Can I collect my race pack on race day?

All race packs must be collected during bib collection times. There is no bib collection available Sunday morning. If you require further assistance, please contact us.

Course access

On course
Are prams permitted on course?

Prams are not permitted in the Marathon or Half Marathon.

Prams are permitted in the 5km, 2km and 1.6km events. Please start at the back of the pack for everyone’s safety and to avoid interfering with other runners.

Are bikes and scooters permitted on course?

No. Bikes and scooters are not permitted on the course at the Yeppoon Running Festival. This helps keep all participants safe.

Are the courses wheelchair accessible?

Yes, the courses are wheelchair accessible, but some sections may be challenging to navigate. If you intend to participate in a wheelchair and require further information or assistance, please contact us.

Entries & transfers

Race Roster
I can’t attend, can I get a refund?

Refunds are not offered unless you selected the Race Roster Enhanced Refund Protection Program during registration. To action a refund under that program, log into your Race Roster registration directly.

If you purchased Enhanced Refund Protection and are having issues, contact Race Roster at support@raceroster.com.

What happens if the race is cancelled?

If the race is cancelled, all entries will be rolled over to the following year so your participation is guaranteed for the next Yeppoon Running Festival.

Can I transfer my entry to another person?

Yes. Entries can be transferred to another person up to 30 days before the race.

  1. Sign into your Race Roster profile.
  2. Select the Transfer option.
  3. Follow the prompts to complete the transfer.

Transfers made more than 21 days before the event incur a $10 admin fee. Within 21 days of the event, the fee increases to $15. All transfers are self-managed through Race Roster.

For help, contact support@raceroster.com.

Can I downgrade to a shorter distance?

Yes. Sign into your Race Roster profile, select the Transfer option, and follow the prompts to change your race distance.

All transfers are self-managed through Race Roster. You may still receive a bib assigned to your original race distance, but the system will be updated to reflect your new distance correctly.

Downgrades made more than 21 days before the event incur a $2 admin fee. Within 21 days of the event, the fee increases to $15. Any price difference will not be refunded.

Can I upgrade to a longer distance?

Yes. Sign into your Race Roster profile, select the Transfer option, and follow the prompts to change your race distance.

  • All transfers are self-managed through Race Roster.
  • You may still receive a bib assigned to your original race distance, but the system will reflect your new distance correctly.
  • Upgrades made more than 21 days before the event incur a $2 admin fee. Within 21 days of the event, the fee increases to $15.
  • You will need to pay the price difference between your original entry and the new distance.

Event services

Extras
I have ordered merchandise, where do I collect this?

Merchandise is collected during race pack collection times. Post-event postage can be arranged as a flat-rate postage fee payable by Eftpos.

View race pack collection

My child is under 12. Can I register them?

Depending on the race distance, YRF may be able to accommodate this. Please contact the team with the distance request. If approved, written permission from the parent or guardian is required.

Contact YRF

Can our club set up a tent at the event?

Yes. Clubs and groups are welcome to register for a designated tent site along the racecourse. It is a great way to support your runners and enjoy the festival atmosphere together.
