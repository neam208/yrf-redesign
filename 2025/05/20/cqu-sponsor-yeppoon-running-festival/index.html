Spotlight on a Long-Standing Sponsor: CQUniversity

Every great event has its champions — those who believe in its potential from the very beginning and stand beside it as it grows. For the Yeppoon Running Festival, CQUniversity has been one of those champions.

Back to News Read Article
Published20th May 2025
CategoryFestival News

Every great event has its champions — those who believe in its potential from the very beginning and stand beside it as it grows. For the Yeppoon Running Festival, CQUniversity has been one of those champions.

Since the festival’s inaugural year, CQUniversity has been more than just a sponsor — they’ve been a steadfast partner, advocate, and integral part of the event’s success story. From the early days of modest crowds to today’s thriving, regionally significant celebration of fitness and community spirit, CQUniversity has supported the festival’s evolution at every step. Their commitment reflects a shared vision: building a healthier, more connected Capricorn Coast.

Over the years, we’ve seen their students and staff line up at the start line, offer recovery support post-race, and cheer on the sidelines — not as outsiders, but as part of the very heartbeat of the festival. Their ongoing presence has helped shape the inclusive, uplifting atmosphere that runners and spectators alike now associate with the Yeppoon Running Festival.

We have asked Elle Cullen, Senior Executive Officer in the Office of the Vice-Chancellor and President, some interesting questions to hear more about what this long-standing partnership means to CQUniversity, and how it ties into their broader mission to foster wellbeing, education and community engagement across Central Queensland.

1. CQUniversity is now a long-term partner of the Yeppoon Running Festival. What does this ongoing sponsorship mean to the university?
Our continued partnership with the Yeppoon Running Festival reflects CQUniversity’s strong commitment to supporting local initiatives that promote health, wellbeing and community connection. We see real value in supporting events that unite the community, inspire personal goals and contribute to the vibrancy of the Capricorn Coast.

5. Will there be CQU students, staff or alumni taking part in this year’s race? Any seasoned runners or first-timers?
Absolutely! Each year, we have a fantastic mix of staff, students and alumni hitting the course. Some are seasoned runners aiming for a PB, while others are walking their first 5K. Our Vice-Chancellor and President is an avid runner too and loves running in the Yeppoon Running Festival!

It’s a great reminder that health and fitness look different for everyone – and that every step counts.

6. Do you have any standout memories or stories from last year’s event – or past festivals – that left a lasting impression?
We always love seeing our CQUniversity physiotherapy students in action, offering recovery support to runners after the race. It’s such a valuable experience for our students to apply their skills in a real-world setting, and the participants really appreciate the chance to get some much-needed recovery and care. It’s a win-win for everyone involved.

CQUniversity’s involvement in the Yeppoon Running Festival is more than just sponsorship — it’s a hands-on celebration of community, education, and active living. We’re proud to have them with us for another incredible year!
