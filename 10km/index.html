10km overview

The YRF CQ Radiology 10km is almost sold out for 2026, giving runners a scenic festival race with the energy of the main event precinct. Runners begin on Anzac Parade and move through Yeppoon's foreshore before returning to the Lagoon finish.

The course keeps the best parts of the destination race experience: ocean views, community support, coastal air, and a strong finish-line atmosphere at Yeppoon Lagoon.

It is ideal for runners chasing speed, building confidence, or stepping into a longer coastal race without taking on the half marathon distance.

Rules & prizes

Course rules

No prams, bikes, scooters, handcycles, crank cycles, or wheelchairs are permitted on course, except race wheelchairs.

Overall winners

Male and female 10km placegetters receive trophies plus cash prizes: 1st $220, 2nd $100, and 3rd $80.

Age categories

1st in each 10km age group receives a Category Winner Medal plus a $40 ProFeet voucher. Categories are U/18, 18-29, 30-39, 40-49, 50-59, 60-69, and 70+.
