What to bring

This is the part people need while standing at collection, so the requirements are separated from the explanatory copy.

Collecting your own pack

Bring your RaceRoster confirmation email. It is emailed to you when you complete registration.

  • RaceRoster confirmation email
  • Enough time to check bib and event details
  • Any purchased event merchandise details

Collecting for someone else

If another person collects your race pack, they need proof that they are authorised to collect it.

  • Copy of the participant registration email
  • Signed letter from the participant giving permission
  • Participant name and event clearly shown

Collection flow

A simple process helps reduce queues and stress for volunteers and runners.

Choose location

Pick Rockhampton on Thursday or Yeppoon on Saturday.

Bring proof

Have your RaceRoster confirmation ready before reaching the desk.

Check pack

Confirm your bib, timing chip, event details, merchandise, and goodies.

Arrive ready

Bring your bib to the start. There is no race-day pickup for standard entries.

Plan collection before race weekend

Race packs include your race bib with timing chip on the back, purchased merchandise, sponsor information, and event goodies. Collecting early helps keep race morning calm.
