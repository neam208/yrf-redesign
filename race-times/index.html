Important reminders

These notes are the highest-risk information on the page, so they get their own cards instead of being buried under the timetable.

Arrive 30 minutes early

Please arrive 30 minutes prior to your race start so you have time to park, warm up, find your start area, and settle in.

No Sunday bib collection

Bibs for all Sunday events must be collected at the bib collection points on Thursday, Friday, or Saturday. Bibs will not be available at the race start.

Collect your race pack before race day

Make the bib collection pathway obvious from the race-times page. This avoids last-minute confusion and gives runners a clear next step.
