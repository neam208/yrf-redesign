Important road closure information

Road closures are managed to protect participants, spectators, and road users while minimising community disruption.

01

Follow traffic controllers

All drivers and pedestrians must follow the instructions of traffic controllers at all times.

02

Allow extra travel time

Plan your trip in advance and allow additional time to reach your destination during closures.

03

Roads reopen when safe

Organisers are committed to reopening roads as quickly as possible once events are completed.
