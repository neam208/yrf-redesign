Race overview

The YRF Bendigo Bank Yeppoon and Emu Park Marathon is built around the natural beauty and coastal character of Yeppoon. Runners begin on Anzac Parade with the ocean as the backdrop before moving through town, creek crossings, coastal roads, and lookout sections.

The course takes in Yeppoon Lagoon, Fig Tree Creek, Appleton Drive, Ross Creek, Scenic Highway, Matthew Flinders Drive, Wreck Point Lookout, Lammermoor Beach, and the Statue Bay turnaround before returning toward Yeppoon.

It is a marathon for runners chasing a personal best and for those who want a memorable destination race with scenery, sea breeze, and strong finish-line energy.

Course rules

No prams, bikes, scooters, handcycles, crank cycles, or wheelchairs are permitted on course, except race wheelchairs.

Overall winners

First, second, and third male and female marathon winners receive trophies and cash prizes. Winners also receive Shokz OpenRun2 headphones.

Age categories

Category winners receive a winner medal and a ProFeet voucher. Categories include U/30 through 70+.
